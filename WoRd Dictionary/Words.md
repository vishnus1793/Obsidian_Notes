1.Acing = Nailing
Usage
- **"Acing a test/exam":** Receiving a perfect score or an 'A' grade.
- **"Acing an interview":** Impressing the interviewers and performing flawlessly.
- **"Acing it":** Succeeding at a difficult task with apparent ease.
