### 3️⃣ Data Privacy ⚠️

|Privacy Factor|PhonePe|Google Pay|Navi UPI|
|---|---|---|---|
|Risk Level|🟡 Moderate|🔴 High|🟡 Moderate-High|
|GPS Required|Sometimes|Yes|Even for basic UPI|

## ⚠️ Key Risks to Know

1. Navi: Forces GPS/location for basic UPI - major privacy red flag
2. Google Pay: Your transaction data feeds Google's ad ecosystem
3. PhonePe: Declining customer support quality; UI changes frustrate users

---

Bottom Line:

- PhonePe for reliability and everyday payments
- Navi if you want loans/investments integrated with payments (but accept privacy trade-offs)
- Google Pay only if you're already in the Google ecosystem and value clean UX over privacy
