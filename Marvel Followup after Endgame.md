# Notes

- "What If...?" is animated but tied to the Multiverse Saga.
- "X-Men '97" is not required for the MCU story but becomes more relevant because of multiverse connections.
- "Deadpool & Wolverine" heavily references older Fox Marvel movies; watching those is optional.
- "I Am Groot" consists of short animated episodes and can be skipped without missing any major story points.
